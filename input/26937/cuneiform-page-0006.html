TAXONOMIC NOTE

Ectoproct (Bryozoa) overwhelmingly dominate the faunas of the Holston Formation. Bryozoan taxonomy has become increasingly com- plex and specialized in recent years (compare Boardman and Cheetham, 1969}. Much of the older taxonomic literature is marginally usable because of the lack of attention to microscopic detail by earlier workers. In the present study, we have used the modern taxonomic studies of Middle Ordovician bryozoans. Fortuitously these treat nearly all the important genera present in the Holston. We have classified to the species level, but in this preliminary report, we treat most of the taxa at generic level.
